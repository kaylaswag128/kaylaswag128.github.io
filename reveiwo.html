Omomo Boba Review

I give it a 9/10 :)

  • Far Location; all the way in Chino Hills :(
  • Not A ton of options
  • Their drinks have perfect ratio of sweet and not-sweet!
  • Good price for ammount of boba and drink
  • Often really busy, but weekdays have short lines!
